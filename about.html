About The Project

Problem Statement

We often find that are drawers are so full that we donâ€™t know what all things are kept and what even exists there. We are even not sure whether a thing is present in the drawers or not. In our fast-moving lives, it becomes utterly difficult to remember and find where our things are present or it even does exists or not. It is also extremely important to keep track of important things like keys, credit cards, debit cards, id cards, cash, etc and check for their inflow and outflow of the drawers.

What we did

What we did is we made an app, which can now help you to organise your stuff by taking the input from you whenever you use your drawer and therefore make an inventory of items with respect to each drawer and also provide you with an image of the item along with the item name using the camera module This product caters to all the people who are forgetful and people who have noone to organise their stuff for them all this was made possible by using firebase and cloudinary for databases , thunkable for app development camera module based on arduino for image capturing,nodemcu for sending data to app via wifi python scripts for uploading images to databases and java for image capturing app
